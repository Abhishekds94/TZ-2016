FAQ
1. What is Prastuti?
- Prastuti is a national level technical paper presentation event.

2. Can a single participant present the paper?
- Yes, either one or two participants can present the paper.

3. What is the fee structure for a single presenter?
- Either the number of presenter is one or two, fee is fixed per paper as mentioned.

4. Will more than two authors get certificate?
- Yes, maximum authors per team are four but only two participants should present their paper.

5. Can I present same paper in two streams?
- No, you cannot present the same paper in two streams but you can present different papers in different streams also you have to pay the fee two times too.

6. Can I apply different papers for the same stream?
- No, you cannot apply different papers for the same stream with same authors, even if you do we select only one paper.

7. Can I apply paper on different topic other than the listed topics?
- Yes, you can apply paper on different topics to the general Email ID of Prastuti provided in the website.

8. Can I present in Prezi?
- No, you cannot. Only Microsoft office PowerPoint must be used for presentation.

9. Will I get participation certificate?
- Yes, certificates will be provided for all the participants.

10. How can I know whether my paper has been selected for presentation or not?
- You will receive a mail from Prastuti team with all details mentioned in it.

11. How can I know when my presentation time slot will be, during Techzone?
- You will receive a mail two days before the TZ, which includes rules, slot and venue of your presentation.
In case you need any changes in the slot provided, inform us as soon as possible.

12. When the results will be announced?
- Results will be announced on the third day of Tz. If all the participants cannot be present on the third day, winners will be intimated about the results and their prizes will be sent by other means.

13. How winners will be decided?
- Winners are decided based on judges’ decision.

14. Will the winners get different certificates?
- Yes, the winners will get a different certificate other than participation certificate.

15. What about accommodation for participants? Is that amount included in the registration fee?
- No, it is not included in registration fee. Accommodation fee is Rs. 100/- per participant. For further details please contact accommodation committee. Contact: Sunith -7204133545

16. Can I register online?
- Yes, you can register through online.

17. I have questions other than this.
- Please feel free to contact the organizers mentioned above.

NOTE:
1. On spot paper submission and selection process is not entertained!
2. Participants who need accommodation should pay 100/- which is fixed per participant for all three days which includes food and accommodation
3. For JNNCE participants food coupons will be provided during their slots.
4. Travelling allowance: One side fare for one participant in a team
5. If there are more than 30 participants from same college (other than JNNCE) in any Tz event, then accommodation will be free of cost for all 30 of them.
6. If there are more than 20 participants from same college (other than JNNCE) in any Tz event, then accommodation cost will be reduced by 50% for all 20 of them.
